# 📖 User Guide - AI Chatbot

Complete guide for using your AI Chatbot with all its features.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Open the Application**
   - Navigate to the chatbot URL
   - You'll see the login page

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Click "Don't have an account? Sign up"
   - Enter your full name
   - Provide your email address
   - Create a secure password
   - Click "Sign Up"

3. **You're In!**
   - Your first conversation is automatically created
   - Start chatting immediately

---

## 💬 Using the Chatbot

### Starting a Conversation

1. **Type Your Message**
   - Use the text input at the bottom
   - Type your question or message

2. **Send Your Message**
   - Press `Enter` to send
   - Or click the send button (➤ icon)
   - Use `Shift + Enter` for a new line

3. **Get AI Response**
   - AI will show a "thinking" animation (•••)
   - Response appears in a few seconds
   - Each message shows a timestamp

### Message Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Ask clear, specific questions
- Provide context when needed
- Use proper grammar for best results

❌ **Don't:**
- Send empty messages
- Send messages while AI is responding

---

## 📂 Managing Conversations

### Creating New Conversations

1. **Click "New Chat"** (+ icon)
   - Located in the sidebar
   - Creates a fresh conversation
   - Previous chats are saved

### Switching Conversations

1. **Click on Any Conversation** in the sidebar
   - Shows all your messages
   - Continues where you left off
   - Messages are auto-saved

### Deleting Conversations

1. **Hover Over a Conversation**
   - Trash icon (🗑️) appears
   - Click to delete
   - Confirms before deleting
   - **Warning:** This cannot be undone!

### Clearing Current Chat

1. **Click "Clear"** in the header
   - Only affects current conversation
   - Confirms before clearing
   - Other conversations remain intact

## ⚙️ Settings & Personalization

### Opening Settings

1. **Click "Settings"** in sidebar
   - Opens settings modal
   - Shows all customization options

### Personal Information

**Update Your Name:**
- Edit the "Name" field
- Click "Save Changes"

**Email:**
- Email is fixed (cannot be changed)
- Used for login only

### Choosing Your Role

**Why It Matters:**
- AI tailors responses to your role
- Different roles get different response styles
- Makes conversations more relevant

**Available Roles:**

1. **Student** 🎓
   - Learning and educational assistance
   - Explanations are detailed and educational
   - Focus on understanding concepts

2. **Professional** 💼
   - Work and career guidance
   - Strategic and actionable advice
   - Business-focused responses

3. **Developer** 💻
   - Programming and technical help
   - Code-focused responses
   - Technical terminology

4. **Researcher** 🔬
   - Research and analysis support
   - Systematic, analytical approach
   - Evidence-based responses

5. **Creative** 🎨
   - Creative and artistic projects
   - Out-of-the-box thinking
   - Artistic perspectives

6. **General** 🌟
   - General purpose assistance
   - Balanced, versatile responses
   - Default role

**How to Select:**
- Click on your preferred role
- Selected role is highlighted
- Click "Save Changes"

### Adding Custom Context

**What Is Context?**
- Additional information about your needs
- Helps AI understand your situation
- Provides more personalized responses

**Examples:**
- "I'm working on a React project and need help with state management"
- "I'm studying for medical exams and need practice questions"
- "I'm writing a science fiction novel about space exploration"
- "I'm planning to start a tech startup in the AI industry"

**How to Add:**
1. Type your context in the text area
2. Be specific and clear
3. Click "Save Changes"
4. AI will consider this in all responses

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### I Can't See My Old Messages

**Solution:**
- Make sure you're logged into the correct account
- Check if you've selected the right conversation
- Messages are saved per conversation

### Sidebar Won't Open on Mobile

**Solution:**
- Look for the menu icon (☰) in the header
- Tap it to toggle sidebar
- Make sure you're not blocking JavaScript

### AI Responses Aren't Relevant

**Solution:**
- Check your role in Settings
- Add more context
- Be more specific in your questions

### Settings Won't Save

**Solution:**
- Click "Save Changes" button
- Check for browser localStorage support
- Try refreshing the page

## 📊 Data & Privacy

### Where Is Your Data Stored?

- **Locally in your browser** (localStorage)
- **Not sent to any server** (unless you integrate an AI backend)
- **Stays on your device**

### What Data Is Saved?

- Your name and email
- Your role and context settings
- All conversations and messages
- Login credentials (locally)

### Data Security

- Passwords stored locally (in production, use backend authentication)
- No data transmission (in current setup)
- Clear browser data to delete all information

## 🆘 Getting Help
